# Gypsy Caravan Escape Warning

## Overview

In [[Game 9]], [[Istvan Vlaszy]] contacts his caravan after the Tremere have taken [[Sabina Premysl]].

## Details

- The caravan knows Sabina was taken by the Tremere but does not know where.
- Istvan warns them to be ready to flee or leave the area because Prague's nights are becoming dangerous.
- The warning follows escalating Tremere, Tzimisce, elder, and Anarch violence.
