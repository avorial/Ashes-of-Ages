# Radomir's Cainite Sales to the Tremere

## Overview

[[Dragan Premysl]] reveals that [[Radomir Pavlovich]] secretly traded Cainites to the Tremere for magical texts, war secrets, and other esoteric knowledge. [[Mira Vlaszy]] later confirms that she helped deliver bodies to the [[Golden Lane Shop]] under Radomir's arrangements.

## Confirmed Details

- Radomir was receiving Tremere magical knowledge, including war-related or warding knowledge.
- The trade involved Cainites or Cainite bodies that Radomir could not openly dispose of.
- Mira served as one of Radomir's communication and delivery agents inside Prague.
- The Prince may not have known the full source of Radomir's knowledge.

## Open Questions

- Exactly how many victims were delivered is not stated.
- Whether Radomir believed the ends justified the means is clear from the testimony, but not a defense of his actions.
