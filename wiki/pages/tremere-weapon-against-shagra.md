# Tremere Weapon Against Shagra

## Overview

At the [[Fireflower Anarch Camp]], the Anarch leaders explain that the Tremere have built a weapon intended to strike the psyche of the ancient Tzimisce force associated with [[Shagra's Bloodline]].

## Details

- The weapon is known by three Tremere.
- It can be empowered by Tremere and carried to the target location.
- The likely target location is the flesh room or corpse-work site associated with Shagra's presence in Prague.
- The coterie considers using [[Sabina as Tremere Leverage]] to convince the Tremere to detonate or deploy the weapon before fleeing.
