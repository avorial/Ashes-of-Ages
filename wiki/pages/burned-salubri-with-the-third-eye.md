# Burned Salubri with the Third Eye

## Overview

In [[Game 7]], the coterie finds a burned vampire in chainmail near a church. Examination reveals fangs and a third eye, pointing to a Salubri.

## Interpretation

- The vampire was likely young, since the body did not immediately ash away completely.
- [[Dragan Premysl]] later says one Salubri warrior was compelled into daylight during the attack on the Tremere Chantry.
