# Gargoyle Attack at Sabina's Cottage

## Event

As [[Sabina Premysl]] finishes drawing the ritual circle and prepares to call the spirit witness, a gargoyle crashes through the ceiling of [[Sabina Premysl's Cottage]] and breaks the ward. The session ends there.

## Implications

- The attack prevents immediate questioning of the spirit witness.
- The timing suggests someone may know the group is following Radomir's ritual trail.
- The attacker may connect to Tremere activity, but the transcript does not confirm this.

## Game 3 resolution

- The gargoyle is destroyed after a hard fight involving Jardani, Theodosia, Father Zemislav, Dalibor, and Istvan.
- Sabina is not saved by the victory; she is taken through the roof afterward.
- The scene leads directly to [[Sabina's Abduction]] and [[Shadow Creature at Sabina's Cottage]].
