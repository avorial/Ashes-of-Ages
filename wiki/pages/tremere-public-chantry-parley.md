# Tremere Public Chantry Parley

## Overview

In [[Game 8]], the coterie emerges near or within the [[Tremere Public Chantry]] and is brought into an apothecary/curiosity-shop front. [[Pierre de la Salle]] speaks for House and Clan Tremere.

## Topics

- The Tremere ask whether the coterie ordered or encouraged [[Dragan Premysl]]'s Chantry raid.
- Pierre offers exchanges of occult knowledge.
- He presents the Tremere as useful to many elders and clans.
- He confirms that the Tremere transform unwanted vampires into loyal or useful servants.
- He claims the Salubri are infernalists and says the Tremere seek their destruction.
