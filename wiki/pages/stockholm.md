# Stockholm

## Overview

Stockholm is the endpoint of Chronicle 1. After roughly a century of travel, survival, and Sabbat formation, the pack lands there and begins inserting itself into the local court.

## Important Events

- The pack arrives after the [[Unconquered Banu Haqim]] incident at sea.
- Stockholm becomes the launch point for Chronicle 2.
- The characters receive a fifty-experience-point time jump and are now solidly ancillae.
