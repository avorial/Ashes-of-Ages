# Prague Curfew and Kindred Travel

## Confirmed facts

- Curfew begins around market close.
- Characters can attempt to move openly with excuses, avoid the town guard, use rooftops, arrange escorts, or lean on contacts.
- [[Jardani Jovonovich]] has Hussite/town guard contacts who can help the coterie move through the city.
- The [[Jewish Quarter]] is not locked like many European Jewish quarters, but Jews seen outside the gates without leaving the city can be arrested.

## Game use

Travel choices matter because the coterie repeatedly moves across the city at night while carrying contraband, following revenant leads, or avoiding attention.
