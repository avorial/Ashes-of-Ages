# Land-Spirit Crossing

## Overview

In [[Game 14]], while portaging the Setite boat, [[Istvan Vlaszy]] hears a voice welcome him. It resembles the pressure of a Tzimisce Methuselah only at first; the transcript clarifies it is something older and more natural.

## Details

- The soil feels different, fine, or silty.
- The weight on Istvan lifts rather than clamps down.
- The phenomenon is not undead.
- The Tzimisce seem to have copied, exploited, or enslaved this type of land-spirit bond elsewhere.
