# Salubri Chantry Raid

## Overview

[[Dragan Premysl]] reveals in [[Game 7]] that he helped smuggle six Salubri warriors into Prague to strike the Tremere. The attack damages Tremere retainers but goes badly.

## Confirmed Details

- One Salubri appears to have been compelled or controlled into daylight and died.
- Dragan is wounded by electricity and by a wooden heart-seeking creature.
- The Tremere later approach the coterie because Dragan was associated with them during Radomir's investigation.
