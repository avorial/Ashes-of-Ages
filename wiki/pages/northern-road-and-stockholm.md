## Overview

After the [[Night of Chaos]], the pack leaves Prague with northern Setites, meets Lithuanian hearth witches, survives a long epilogue, and reaches [[Stockholm]].

## Destination

Stockholm is where Chronicle 2 begins. The pack arrives older, fully Sabbat, and carrying knowledge of Fireflower, land-spirits, Tremere hubris, Cain's tomb rumors, and the consequences of Prague.
