# Adonis's Island Refuge

## Overview

[[Gregory]] says he knows an ancient Greek Gangrel named [[Adonis]] who lives on an island in the river. The implication is that running water interferes with Shagra's pervasive influence, making the island a possible refuge or blind spot.
