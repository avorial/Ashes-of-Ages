# Svigar Derkinson

## Overview

Svigar Derkinson is the tall, thin speaker of the [[Norse Setite Anteaters]] at the [[Fireflower Anarch Camp]].

## Appears In

- [[Game 13]]

## Details

- His aura is interwoven with spirits and sorceries that guard him.
- He speaks of the Fireflower as stolen Promethean flame and a weapon against elder control.
- He describes northern darkness, sleeping dragons, and a tomb that may be Cain's.
- He invites the coterie north on the [[Setite Ship North]].

## Relationships

- [[Ursa]], [[Colleen Norgobane]], and [[Thor Skydre]] - companions.
