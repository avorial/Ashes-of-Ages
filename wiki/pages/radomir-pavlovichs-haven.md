# Radomir Pavlovich's Haven

## Overview

The haven of [[Radomir Pavlovich]], found after his destruction. A sale notice is nailed to the door, and the property is later purchased by Dalibor through retainers.

## Important events

- The coterie searches the house in [[Game 1]].
- They find revenant blood, ritual markings, missing books, correspondence, [[Stone of Hearts Wandering]], and the [[Bronze Fleshcrafted Vampire Statue]].
- The house is later targeted by arson; a suspect is taken to [[Castle Jail]].
- [[Istvan Vlaszy]] was the second-highest bidder at auction.
