# Cain's Northern Tomb

## Overview

The [[Norse Setite Anteaters]] tell the coterie of a northern tomb that legend identifies as Cain's own resting place, or at least a place of ancient vampiric power.

## Details

- The tomb is associated with mazes, contraptions, and places where sunlight never reaches.
- It may contain a basalt throne.
- Those who enter are said not to return.
- The Setites hope [[The Librarian]] or [[The Architect]] might help bypass the tomb's mysteries.

## Uncertain

- The transcript does not confirm whether this is truly Cain's tomb.
