# Jewish Quarter Hidden Yeshiva

## Overview

The coterie is led through the Jewish Quarter to a merchant house with a butcher-shop front and a hidden stairway. Behind the false frontage is a larger learning hall or yeshiva where [[Prince Josef Zvi]] receives them in an obfuscated/masked appearance.
