# Gregory

## Overview

Gregory is a Ventrue encountered during a card game at the [[Three Keys Tavern]]. He dislikes [[Prince Josef Zvi]] but is cautious rather than openly rebellious.

## Information Shared

- A raid against outside forces is planned in three days.
- Faithful vampire hunters are south of Prague.
- The mortal sheriff resists mental persuasion.
- [[Adonis]] lives on a river island.
