# Mortal Sheriff of Prague

## Overview

The mortal sheriff of Prague accepts a corpse from [[Dalibor]] and offers information on other criminals in need of justice.

## Notes

- [[Gregory]] says the sheriff resists mental persuasion by vampires.
- He points Dalibor toward [[Wilhelm of the Three Keys]].
