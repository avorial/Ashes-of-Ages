# Radomir Pavlovich Investigation

## Confirmed facts

- [[Radomir Pavlovich]] was a Tzimisce elder in Prague and a representative/contact point for Tzimisce voivodes outside the city.
- His destruction is serious enough that [[Alessandro di Ravello]] summons the coterie and gives them a mandate to investigate.
- The attack occurred at [[Radomir Pavlovich's Haven]].
- Radomir had multiple revenants under his control, including protectors and couriers/communications servants.
- [[Sabina Premysl]] says Radomir was occupied by a ritual involving spirits when he died, and believes the ritual was intended to lower his defenses.

## Leads

- [[Istvan Vlaszy]] carried packages for Radomir and knows of a courier route to a [[Remote Tzimisce Castle]].
- [[Dragan Premysl]] and [[Sabina Premysl]] are direct witnesses to Radomir's household and research.
- [[Radomir's Missing Ritual Book]] appears central to the murder.
- [[Tremere-Tzimisce War]] correspondence makes Tremere involvement plausible but unconfirmed.
- A [[Mysterious Crusader Agent]] or less-than-human crusader presence may be connected.

## Theories

- The ritual package may have been bait sent to Radomir by an enemy who knew his interests.
- The Tremere may be involved, but the transcript preserves this as suspicion rather than proof.
- The gargoyle attack suggests the conspirators may be monitoring attempts to contact the spirit witness.
