# Pack Roles After Prague

## Overview

At the end of [[Game 14]], the group clarifies that the coterie is now a Sabbat pack rather than merely an Anarch-adjacent coterie.

## Discussed Roles

- [[Father Zemislav]] - pack priest.
- [[Istvan Vlaszy]] - likely pack leader, especially after becoming an ancilla Banu Haqim.
- [[Katherine Karalova]] - possible abbot because her Nosferatu instincts could make the haven warren-like and secure.
- [[Theodosia Byzantios]] - possible abbot or seer figure, though her instability and Tzimisce taint complicate leadership.

## Notes

The transcript leaves final role assignment partly open for later play.
