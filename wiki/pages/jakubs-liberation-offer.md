# Jakub's Liberation Offer

## Overview

At the [[Red Calf]], [[Jakub of Thessaloniki (Zakariya ibn Masud al-Muntazir)]] says he shot or marked the coterie not to kill them, but to test or liberate them. He calls himself a liberator and speaks against the chains of the blood bond.

## Key Claims

- He attempted to liberate [[Istvan Vlaszy]].
- He believes proving willingness to fight, walk away, or take responsibility is part of the path away from bondage.
- He frames the coterie as enslaved by those who sent them to find Radomir's killer.
