# Tzimisce Revenant Network

## Confirmed facts

- Radomir used revenants as both protectors and messengers.
- Revenant blood is supernatural and does not decay like normal mortal blood in the same way.
- The coterie learns the Premysl family served Radomir closely.
- [[Dragan Premysl]] acted as an enforcer/protector.
- [[Sabina Premysl]] served as a spirit-vessel in Radomir's research.
- [[Istvan Vlaszy]] served as a courier for Radomir's packages.

## Notes

- The transcripts state that revenants primarily serve the Tzimisce, though some may be traders or otherwise outside direct household service.
- If no Tzimisce claims Radomir's revenants, they may become available to those with the means to employ or protect them.
