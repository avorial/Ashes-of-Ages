# Mira Vlaszy's House

## Overview

The coterie visits [[Mira Vlaszy]] after a Cappadocian negotiator produces an ear said to be hers. Mira is alive and still has both ears.

## Important Events

- Mira explains that the ear had been surrendered earlier under Radomir's Tremere arrangements.
- She confirms deliveries to the [[Golden Lane Shop]].
- She explains Radomir's inside-city and outside-city communication network.
