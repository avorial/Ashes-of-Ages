# Butcher Shop of the Impossible River

## Overview

The butcher shop is the apparent source of [[Impossible River]]. [[Eliska von Vlasak]] buys meat there that later appears to be the heart of her dead husband.

## Investigation

- The river seems to flow from a chute or basement access.
- Something appears to have been pushed down the chute within the last week or so.
- Deeper investigation leads toward the [[Tremere Public Chantry]].
