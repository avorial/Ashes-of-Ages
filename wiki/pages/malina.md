# Malina

## Overview

Malina is a werewolf recognized by description as a notorious figure of the region. She ambushes [[Jardani Jovonovich]] in Prague, transforms into war form, but does not try to kill him.

## Relationships

- [[Jardani Jovonovich]] - lover.

## Notes

- Her presence inside Prague is considered alarming because werewolves normally avoid cities.
- Jardani initially tries to conceal her identity from the group.
