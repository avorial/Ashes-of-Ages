# Sabina Premysl's Cottage

## Overview

A small one-story cottage with a thatched roof, frosted poor-quality glass, and a rope-knot knocker. The Gauntlet is extremely thin here.

## Important events

- Theodosia is overwhelmed by nature spirits.
- Eliska experiences sorrow and graveyard impressions through synesthesia.
- The coterie sees many awakened or spirit-bound household objects.
- [[Sabina Premysl]] explains Radomir's ritual research.
- A gargoyle crashes through the roof and breaks the ritual ward.
