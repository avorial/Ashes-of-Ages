# Remote Tzimisce Castle

## Overview

A castle four hours north of Prague connected to [[Istvan Vlaszy]]'s courier work for [[Radomir Pavlovich]]. Istvan met a page outside town and rode there as part of a package/message route.

## Open questions

- Who controls the castle?
- What was carried there?
- Whether the route is connected to Radomir's murder remains uncertain.

## Game 4 notes

- Istvan reaches the castle during the day to deliver Radomir's package.
- Four Hussite-style war wagons with red-capped troops attack shortly afterward.
- Tzimisce at the castle tell Istvan that Tremere agents have manipulated some Hussite bands into attacking Tzimisce holdings.
