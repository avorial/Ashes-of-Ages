# Adonis

## Overview

Adonis is described by [[Gregory]] as an ancient Greek Gangrel living on a river island near Prague. Gregory says Adonis has the regal aspect of an eagle and was a speaker of his clan in Rome before the Republic.

## Notes

- The river island may be protected from or resistant to [[Shagra]]'s pervasive influence.
