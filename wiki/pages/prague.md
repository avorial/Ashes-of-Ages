# Prague

## Overview

The first two sessions take place in Prague in 1433, during the Hussite crisis. Mortal politics, curfew, crusader pressure, and Kindred elder politics all shape movement through the city.

## District notes

- The castle district lies across the river and includes wealthy makers and high-status places.
- The western part of Old Town is the [[Jewish Quarter]].
- The map marks Elysium-like locations with skulls, the university with a book, late markets with coins, and known Tremere space with a tower.
