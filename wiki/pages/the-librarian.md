# The Librarian

## Overview

The Librarian is a Nosferatu information broker or elder associated with Prague's hidden archives. The transcript connects him to [[Katherine Karalova]] as her sire or patron, though exact wording should be treated cautiously.

## Appears In

- [[Game 3]] - approached through Elysium circles after Sabina's abduction.
- [[Game 4]] - meets the coterie in the library and explains Prince Josef's history.

## Relationships

- [[Katherine Karalova]] - sire or close Nosferatu patron, based on table phrasing.
- [[Prince Josef Zvi]] - knows important history and can arrange a meeting.

## Associated Locations

- [[The Librarian's Library]]
- [[Castle Catacombs]]

## Notes

- He says the Prince may listen if shown that his agents are escalating Prague toward war.
- He accepts the [[Book of Koldun Exorcery]] as payment for arranging the audience.
