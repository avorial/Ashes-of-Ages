# What Happened to Prague

## Before The Collapse

Prague begins as a tense city under [[Prince Josef Zvi]], [[Alessandro di Ravello]], and elder supervision. The Hussite crisis gives mortal cover to supernatural panic, and [[Radomir Pavlovich]]'s destruction exposes Tremere, Tzimisce, revenant, and spirit conflicts beneath the city.

## Breaking Points

- [[Sabina Premysl]] is taken and later Embraced by the Tremere off screen.
- The coterie exposes Cainite sales to the Tremere.
- The Fireflower gives younger vampires a way to escape elder blood bonds.
- Hidden Salubri aid makes the [[Orlok Assassination]] possible.
- The [[Gunpowder Diversion at Elder Elysium]] escalates the Anarch assault.

## Aftermath

- [[Adrastos von Orlok]] is dead.
- Toreador, Lasombra, Cappadocian, and other elder networks are destabilized.
- Shagra's influence remains a threat rather than a solved problem.
- The coterie leaves before seeing the full city outcome.
- Later news in [[Game 14]] says the Anarchs lose the wider war and the Camarilla forms.
