# Radomir Pavlovich

## Overview

Radomir Pavlovich was a Tzimisce elder in Prague and the representative/contact point for powerful Tzimisce voivodes outside the city. His destruction before Game 1 is the inciting crisis of the chronicle.

## Appears in

- [[Game 1]] - named by [[Alessandro di Ravello]] as the murdered elder.
- [[Game 2]] - his revenants and ritual research become the focus of the investigation.

## Relationships

- [[Sabina Premysl]] - used by Radomir as a vessel for spirit-communion research.
- [[Dragan Premysl]] - protector/enforcer loyal to Radomir.
- [[Istvan Vlaszy]] - courier for Radomir's packages.
- [[Borislav Premysl]] - revenant servant associated with his household.
- [[Prince Josef Zvi]] - corresponded with Radomir about Tremere domain.

## Game 3-4 notes

- [[Dragan Premysl]] and [[Mira Vlaszy]] reveal that Radomir traded Cainites or bodies to the Tremere for magical secrets.
- His work may have been connected to [[Prince Joseph and Antediluvian Influence]], but the crimes remain his own.
- Sabina's abduction may be tied to knowledge Radomir tried to transmit through spirits.
