# Land Spirits And Tzimisce Dominion

## Overview

Chronicle 1 repeatedly ties vampiric power to land: Tzimisce soil, revenant service, Shagra's influence, and the spiritual geography beneath Prague. [[Game 14]] reframes that pattern by showing [[Devara]] and the [[Lithuanian Hearth Pact]], where vampires marry the land rather than enslaving it.

## Working Distinction

- Tzimisce dominion: land and spirits forced into service.
- Hearth pact: land treated as spouse, partner, and host.
- Shagra: an ancient force that may exploit or corrupt the land-bound channel.
