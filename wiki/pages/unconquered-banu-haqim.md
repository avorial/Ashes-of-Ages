# Unconquered Banu Haqim

## Overview

During the voyage to [[Stockholm]], [[Istvan Vlaszy]] feels the pressure of the Tremere curse placed on the Assamites/Banu Haqim.

## Event

- Istvan feels compelled to submit.
- He fights it.
- He coughs up all his blood as a viscous, spiky creature.
- The pack restrains and feeds him while others fight the manifested curse off the boat.
- The Viniculum protects him where many Banu Haqim without such bonds find vitae poisonous.

## Meaning

Istvan is described as one of the Unconquered: broken from old bindings, but unbound and unbroken by the Tremere curse.
