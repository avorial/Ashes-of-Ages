# Hussite Crypt Penance Rite

## Overview

[[Father Zemislav]] witnesses a severe Hussite rite in which a greedy or corrupt man publicly confesses sins, manipulations of church authority, and use of heresy accusations against enemies. The crowd accepts a penitential demand that he be sealed in a grave or crypt for three days.

## Confirmed Details

- A cup is passed among the crowd.
- The man is sealed away in imitation of Christ's three days in the tomb.
- The rite appears socially and religiously charged; its supernatural status is not confirmed.
