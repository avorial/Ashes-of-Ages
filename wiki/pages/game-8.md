# Game 8

## Synopsis

Game 8 begins on April 14, 1433, with the coterie weighing how soon to act before the expected attack. [[Father Zemislav]] spreads a rumor that the failed crypt miracle was a Hussite extremist plot and begins tying his sire [[Alessandro di Ravello]] to the extremist faction, risking the Masquerade to weaken him politically.

[[Eliska von Vlasak]] sees an impossible river running through the street. No one else sees it normally, though others later smell or sense something. The river leads to the [[Butcher Shop of the Impossible River]], where she buys meat that changes into what appears to be the heart of her dead husband once she carries it away.

The group investigates the butcher shop and descends into a disturbing hidden space. The shadows themselves feel afraid. Deeper inside, the coterie realizes they are near or within the [[Tremere Public Chantry]].

The Tremere bring them into a public apothecary/curiosity-shop front, where [[Pierre de la Salle]] speaks for the Chantry. He denies that the coterie ordered Dragan's raid, offers knowledge-for-knowledge exchanges, describes Tremere work against Salubri "infernalists," and openly confirms that the Tremere convert unwanted vampires into useful servants. He says multiple elders, including members of the coterie's own lineages, have delivered unwanted Cainites to the Tremere.

After the meeting, the coterie discusses taking the [[Fireflower and Proto-Vauldere]] and begins planning action against elder targets, including [[Adrastos von Orlok]], [[Alessandro di Ravello]], and possibly the Tremere themselves.
