# Tremere-Hussite War Wagons

## Overview

After [[Istvan Vlaszy]] delivers Radomir's package to a [[Remote Tzimisce Castle]], four Hussite-style war wagons with red-capped soldiers attack the site.

## Confirmed Details

- War wagons are associated with Hussite military tactics.
- The red caps suggest Tremere-linked servants or messengers.
- Tzimisce at the castle believe the Tremere have blood-bound or manipulated some roving Hussite bands into attacking Tzimisce castles.
