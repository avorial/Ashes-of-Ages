# Elder Targets Plot

## Overview

After the Tremere parley in [[Game 8]], the coterie openly discusses taking the [[Fireflower and Proto-Vauldere]] and acting against elder targets to create internal disruption before outside forces intervene.

## Named Targets

- [[Adrastos von Orlok]]

- Potentially the Tremere themselves.

## Timing

The group considers coordinating action with the expected raid in roughly three days.
