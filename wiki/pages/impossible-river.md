# Impossible River

## Overview

In [[Game 8]], [[Eliska von Vlasak]] sees a river running through a Prague street where no river should be. Others do not see it normally, though later some can smell or sense something.

## Properties

- It feels firm or resistant when touched.
- It does not leave Eliska's shoe wet.
- It flows from the direction of the [[Butcher Shop of the Impossible River]] toward the city gates.
- Folklore connects such rivers to fate, moonlight, lost dead, and places of personal significance.

## Personal Omen

Eliska buys meat from the butcher shop; away from the river, it appears as the heart of her dead husband.
