# Awakened Spirit Objects

## Confirmed facts

- At [[Sabina Premysl's Cottage]], the barrier between the physical and spirit worlds is extremely thin.
- Theodosia perceives nature spirits and is overwhelmed.
- The house contains many objects that were experimented on to awaken or capture spirits.
- Furniture and household objects respond subtly; chairs move into place as guests sit.
- Sabina keeps failures in the basement.

## Related concepts

- The transcript compares temporary awakened objects to talens and stronger multi-use objects to fetishes.
- These are spirit/nature phenomena, not wraiths or the dead.
