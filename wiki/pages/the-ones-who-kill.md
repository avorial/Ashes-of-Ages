# The Ones Who Kill

## Overview

The shadow creature at [[Sabina Premysl's Cottage]] says Radomir's killer is connected to "the ones who kill." The phrase becomes a clue rather than a solved identity.

## Uses In Play

- [[Istvan Vlaszy]] links it to the crusader-like crossbow bolt.
- The trail leads to Jacob at the [[Red Calf]].
- The phrase may point to Assamite/Banu Haqim assassins, crusader-linked agents, or another network; the transcript does not settle this.
