# Istvan's Banu Haqim Embrace

## Overview

At the [[Fireflower Anarch Camp]], [[Jakub of Thessaloniki (Zakariya ibn Masud al-Muntazir)]] performs a ritual Embrace for [[Istvan Vlaszy]] after debate over whether a revenant or mortal slave can take part in the new rites.

## Details

- Jakub uses a potion or ritual call to the blood of Assamites from far away.
- Istvan receives visions of Assamites, Alamut, and Cain.
- The Embrace breaks the old Tzimisce revenant silence and turns Istvan into a Banu Haqim/Assamite.
- Later, the pack's Viniculum helps him survive the clan curse, making him one of the [[Unconquered Banu Haqim]].
