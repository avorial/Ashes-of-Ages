# Golden Lane Shop

## Overview

The Golden Lane Shop is identified by [[Mira Vlaszy]] as the place where she delivered bodies or victims for [[Radomir Pavlovich]]'s Tremere dealings.

## Important Events

- Mira names it during her testimony in [[Game 4]].
- It is tied to [[Radomir's Cainite Sales to the Tremere]].
