# Tremere Public Chantry

## Overview

The public Tremere Chantry appears as an apothecary and curiosity shop during the day. It includes a parlor used for formal meetings.

## Important Events

- [[Pierre de la Salle]] meets the coterie there in [[Game 8]].
- The Tremere use it to exchange knowledge and information with other clans.

## Chronicle 1 finale notes

- The Anarch plan in [[Game 11]] centers on forcing or luring the Tremere to use the [[Tremere Weapon Against Shagra]].
- [[Sabina as Tremere Leverage]] is discussed because Sabina's off-screen Tremere Embrace may let her argue that Shagra can pursue the Tremere even if they flee Prague.
