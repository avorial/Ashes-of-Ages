# Marzanna

## Confirmed facts

- A book in [[Radomir Pavlovich's Haven]] describes a person claiming to be the channel of Marzanna, named as a Slavic god of winter and death.
- The channel claimed that burning them would only make them stronger and that cold winds would kill crops.
- Characters with survival knowledge know crops in the area have become harder to grow for the last seven to ten years.

## Unconfirmed

- The transcript does not prove that Marzanna is responsible for crop failures. It presents the connection as an ominous clue.
