# Game 7

## Synopsis

Game 7 opens with preparations and personal moves. [[Dalibor]] kills a released criminal and brings the body to the mortal sheriff, beginning a justice-for-favors relationship. The sheriff points him toward [[Wilhelm of the Three Keys]], an innkeeper accused of imprisoning and forcing marriage on a woman.

[[Theodosia Byzantios]] discovers that her contact with Tzimisce blood has changed her: she can use Vicissitude and wakes with an altered voice. [[Katherine Karalova]] begins cataloging books from her sire's library for later removal.

At the [[Night Market]], [[Jardani Jovonovich]] is ambushed by a woman who transforms into a war-form werewolf but does not try to kill him. Later discussion reveals she is [[Malina]], Jardani's lover, and that her presence in Prague is extremely dangerous.

The coterie also finds a burned vampire in chainmail with a third eye, almost certainly a young Salubri who died in the sun. [[Dragan Premysl]] appears wounded and shaken, explaining that he smuggled Salubri warriors into Prague to attack the Tremere, but one was compelled into daylight and a wooden heart-seeking creature pursued him.

The group then visits the [[Three Keys Tavern]], where [[Gregory]] joins a card game and reveals useful court gossip: a raid is planned in three days, faithful vampire hunters are south of the city, the mortal sheriff resists mental persuasion, and an ancient Gangrel named [[Adonis]] lives on a river island beyond Shagra's easy reach.
