# Lasombra Founder's Death

## Overview

Game 3 and Game 4 discuss a recent event among the Lasombra: a group involving Lasombra rebels, Assamite/Banu Haqim aid, and Tzimisce ritual knowledge broke elder bonds and killed a foundational Lasombra figure.

## Confirmed From Transcript

- Father Zemislav connects this to a vision involving day, night, and a black sun.
- Jacob says the Keepers have been liberated, though they still keep slaves of their own.
- Out-of-character clarification identifies this as the death of the Lasombra Antediluvian/founder and links it to Gratiano and allied assassins.

## Caution

Some of this page is table clarification rather than clean in-character testimony.
