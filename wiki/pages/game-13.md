# Game 13

## Synopsis

In the Anarch camp, the coterie meets the [[Norse Setite Anteaters]], a northern heretical Setite band led by [[Svigar Derkinson]] with [[Ursa]], [[Colleen Norgobane]], and [[Thor Skydre]]. They have already taken the Fireflower and intend to return north, spreading revolt and seeking secrets such as [[Cain's Northern Tomb]].

The group discusses whether to return to Prague, recover [[Katherine's Hidden Trunk]], or flee before Shagra and the elders answer. A distant explosion marks the [[Gunpowder Diversion at Elder Elysium]]. The coterie chooses to travel with the Setites toward Lithuania and the Teutonic lands aboard the [[Setite Ship North]].

On the road, crusader attackers and Tremere redcaps ambush a carriage. A Tremere calling himself [[Greg the Tremere]] emerges, carries Hermetic tools and fetishes, and demonstrates overwhelming elemental power in the [[Boat That Sailed on Land]]. He gives the [[Tremere Origin Confession]], claiming the Tremere altered Tzimisce vitae to free it from spiritual taint and then took the Salubri curse into themselves.
