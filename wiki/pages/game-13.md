# Game 13

## Session Synopsis

At the Anarch camp, the pack meets the [[Norse Setite Anteaters]], a northern Setite band led by [[Svigar Derkinson]] with [[Ursa]], [[Colleen Norgobane]], and [[Thor Skydre]]. They have taken the Fireflower and plan to carry revolt back north, where they speak of sleeping dragons, long darkness, and [[Cain's Northern Tomb]].

The pack debates returning to Prague for [[Katherine's Hidden Trunk]] or leaving before Shagra and the elders answer. A distant explosion marks the [[Gunpowder Diversion at Elder Elysium]]. The pack chooses the road north with the Setites.

On the road, crusaders and Tremere redcaps attack a carriage. A Tremere calling himself [[Greg the Tremere]] emerges, reveals tremendous power, and gives the [[Tremere Origin Confession]]. His magic produces the [[Boat That Sailed on Land]].

## Major Events

- The pack meets the northern Setites.
- [[Cain's Northern Tomb]] is introduced as a northern mythic target.
- The Elder Elysium explosion begins in the distance.
- The pack leaves Prague.
- Greg appears after the [[Redcap Ambush on the Road]].

## Character Moments

- Katherine weighs whether to recover or leave her hidden trunk.
- Istvan recognizes the Setites as a heretical northern branch.
- The pack begins thinking as a pack rather than as separate Prague survivors.

## New Lore

- [[Norse Setite Anteaters]]

## Consequences

- Prague is left behind.
- The northern road becomes the bridge to Chronicle 2.
- Tremere history becomes more complicated than simple enemy propaganda.
