# Mira Vlaszy's Testimony

## Overview

[[Mira Vlaszy]] tells the coterie that she acted as one of [[Radomir Pavlovich]]'s inside-Prague messengers. She delivered bodies to the [[Golden Lane Shop]] and confirms that her ear had previously been given under Radomir's arrangements, not freshly cut by the Cappadocian negotiator.

## Key Points

- Mira worked Radomir's court communications inside Prague.
- [[Istvan Vlaszy]] handled outside-city communications.
- Mira believes the Prince may not have known the source of Radomir's Tremere knowledge.
- She believes [[Sabina Premysl]] may have been taken because the knowledge she carried was valuable.
- She links Radomir's work to efforts around [[Prince Joseph and Antediluvian Influence]].
