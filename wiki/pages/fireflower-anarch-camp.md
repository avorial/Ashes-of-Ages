# Fireflower Anarch Camp

## Overview

The Fireflower Anarch camp outside Prague becomes the coterie's refuge after the [[Orlok Assassination]].

## Important Events

- The coterie meets [[Jakub of Thessaloniki (Zakariya ibn Masud al-Muntazir)]], [[Graziano de Veronese]], and [[Micah Vykos]].
- [[Istvan's Banu Haqim Embrace]] occurs there.
- [[Father Zemislav]] leads the [[First Fireflower Valderie]] with the [[Silver Fireflower Chalice]].
- The camp discusses the [[Tremere Weapon Against Shagra]] and the [[Gunpowder Diversion at Elder Elysium]].
- [[Norse Setite Anteaters]] are present before the coterie flees north.
