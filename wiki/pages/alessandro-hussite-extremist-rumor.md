# Alessandro Hussite Extremist Rumor

## Overview

In [[Game 8]], [[Father Zemislav]] spreads a rumor that the failed burial miracle was a plot by Hussite extremists. He then begins connecting his sire [[Alessandro di Ravello]] to the extremist faction.

## Risk

Zemislav is willing to risk the Masquerade to compromise Alessandro, but the immediate roll suggests mortals do not realize Alessandro is a vampire.
