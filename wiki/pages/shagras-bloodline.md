# Shagra's Bloodline

## Overview

Shagra is the name used for the Tzimisce Methuselah influence tied to [[Radomir Pavlovich]], [[Istvan Vlaszy]], and later [[Theodosia Byzantios]]. Theodosia perceives the influence as spread through Prague itself, present in blood, skin, mist, plants, and people.

## Confirmed Details

- Radomir's stored blood carries a tangible Methuselah presence.
- Theodosia experiences the influence as invasive and nearly soul-consuming.
- Istvan believes Prince Josef's arm ritual exposed his true ancient connection.
- The group theorizes that Radomir may have been trying to help the Prince resist or understand this influence.
