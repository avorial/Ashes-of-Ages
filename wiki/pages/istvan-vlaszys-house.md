# Istvan Vlaszy's House

## Overview

A transport-company-like house, described as a 14th-century equivalent of a trunk and message transport business. The door has a wooden eagle-claw knocker.

## Important events

- The coterie questions [[Istvan Vlaszy]] here about his bid for Radomir's haven and his courier work.
- Istvan reveals a package route involving a page outside Prague and a castle four hours north.
