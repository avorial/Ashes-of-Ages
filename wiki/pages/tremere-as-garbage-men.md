# Tremere as Garbage Men

## Overview

During the parley in [[Game 8]], [[Pierre de la Salle]] openly frames the Tremere as accepting a role like garbage men: taking unwanted Cainites from elders and turning them into something useful rather than merely destroying them.

## Confirmed By Pierre

- Multiple elders and clans have brought unwanted vampires to the Tremere.
- Dalibor's sire has brought broodmates to them.
- The right hand of the Prince and Father Zemislav's sire are also implicated in asking the Tremere for harsh justice.
