# St. Petersburg Sabbat Work

## Overview

In the fast epilogue of [[Game 14]], the pack reaches the court of St. Petersburg during the height of Brujah influence.

## Details

- Brujah there view absolute nobility and serfdom as a reflection of Cain's command over mortals.
- Blood flows freely because mortal lives are treated cheaply.
- The pack converts large groups of Brujah to its cause.
- They encourage or manipulate ghouling among Russian nobles, leading toward violent wars and attacks on neighbors.

## Reward / Growth

- The group receives a free out-of-clan discipline opportunity from this period.
