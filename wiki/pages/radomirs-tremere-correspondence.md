# Radomir's Tremere Correspondence

## Overview

Correspondence found at [[Radomir Pavlovich's Haven]] documents Radomir's hostility toward Clan Tremere and his desire to expel or purge them from Prague.

## Extracted facts

- Radomir chronicled Tremere movement and activity in the city.
- He searched for the Tremere primary chantry.
- Replies from [[Prince Josef Zvi]] refused to facilitate attacks on Tremere domain.
