# Necromancer Countermeasures

## Overview

At [[The Librarian's Library]], [[Istvan Vlaszy]] researches necromancers. The notes include an account of Adolfo Giovanni killing his Cappadocian sire by filling zombies with hard liquor and igniting trails, as well as references to mortal necromancers who can paralyze vampiric necromancers during casting.

## Usefulness

This is practical research rather than a resolved plot point. It suggests that mortal necromancers may be dangerous to Cainite necromancers and are often hunted in Christian lands.
