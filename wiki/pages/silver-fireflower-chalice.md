# Silver Fireflower Chalice

## Overview

[[Father Zemislav]] crafts a silver chalice to hold the blood of the [[First Fireflower Valderie]].

## Details

- The chalice forms to fit Zemislav's hand.
- It is associated with silver stakes or silver implements used in the ritual.
- Blood placed in it becomes the focus for the Fireflower manifestation.
