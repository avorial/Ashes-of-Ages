# Mysterious Crusader Agent

## Confirmed facts

- In Game 2, a follower or attacker traces from the [[Neonate Elysium]] area and fires a crossbow bolt.
- The bolt is identified as coming from trees in German lands.
- The group connects this to the crusader forces attacking the Hussites.
- Istvan/Dragan-side discussion mentions a less-than-human crusader presence that has entered the city.

## Unconfirmed

- The identity, clan, master, and exact allegiance of this agent are not established in the transcript.
