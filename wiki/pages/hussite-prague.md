# Hussite Prague

## Overview

Prague in 1433 is shaped by the aftermath of Jan Hus's execution and the rise of Hussite reform in Bohemia. The transcripts frame the city as divided between Catholic crusader pressure, Hussite resistance, and internal Hussite splits between moderates and radicals.

## In play

- [[Jardani Jovonovich]] supports the Hussite movement as a Brujah.
- [[Father Zemislav]] is a former Catholic inquisitor and current Hussite priest.
- A radical Hussite meeting is scheduled at [[South Market Square]] at Vespers.
- Crusader forces from German lands are connected to later clues, including the crossbow bolt in Game 2.
