# Game 9

## Synopsis

After the Tremere parley, the coterie weighs whether to strike [[Garinol]] or [[Adrastos von Orlok]]. [[Istvan Vlaszy]] warns his caravan that Prague is becoming unsafe, then finds [[Borislav Premysl]] at [[Dragan Premysl's House]] and learns that Dragan is reshaping prisoners into [[Dragan's Szlachta Work|szlachta]] under an influence he does not fully control.

[[Katherine Karalova]] researches [[Garinol Intelligence|Garinol's history, feeding pattern, and corpse-house interests]]. At the [[Night Market]], the group intervenes when a wanted alchemist is seized, and Katherine later discovers [[Rudolph the Clockwork Alchemist]] wounded in a Nosferatu oubliette.

The coterie follows Dragan's directions to the [[Hidden Salubri Cave]]. The Salubri agree to help against Orlok, but speak in guarded terms about names, payment, and the danger of Tremere control. In the deeper cave, [[Theodosia Byzantios]] and [[Father Zemislav]] look into the [[Labyrinth Chasm]] and glimpse a zero-shroud wound into the underworld. On the way out, the group finds [[Carrion Footprints at the River|carrion-smelling footprints]] that suggest someone followed them from the water.
