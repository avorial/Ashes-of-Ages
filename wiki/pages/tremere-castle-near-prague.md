# Tremere Castle Near Prague

## Overview

A Tremere-influenced castle or holding near the road associated with Istvan's courier knowledge. It is described as roughly three miles to the left if one takes the wrong road.

## Importance

- The location reinforces Tremere suspicion in the [[Radomir Pavlovich Investigation]].
- The transcript does not prove that the Tremere used this site in the murder.
