# Game 12 Missing Transcript Summary

## Status

No Game 12 transcript was provided with the Chronicle 1 finale files. This page preserves the continuity gap and only records facts stated in the opening recap of [[Game 13]].

## Recap-Derived Events

- The coterie woke in the [[Fireflower Anarch Camp]] and learned their actions had started the [[Night of Chaos]].
- Multiple elders were dead and Prague's power balance was unstable.
- [[Graziano de Veronese]] challenged whether [[Istvan Vlaszy]], still mortal/revenant at that moment, could join the new rites.
- [[Jakub of Thessaloniki (Zakariya ibn Masud al-Muntazir)]] performed [[Istvan's Banu Haqim Embrace]] through a ritual potion tied to distant Assamite blood, Alamut, and Cain.
- [[Father Zemislav]] crafted the [[Silver Fireflower Chalice]] and led the [[First Fireflower Valderie]].
- The group spoke with Anarch leaders about [[Micah Vykos]], Shagra's influence through land magic, the [[Tremere Weapon Against Shagra]], and the plan for an explosion at Elder Elysium.
- Fleeing Prague became a practical alternative to continuing the fight inside the city.
