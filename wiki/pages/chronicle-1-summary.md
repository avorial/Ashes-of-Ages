# Chronicle 1 Summary

## The Radomir Investigation

The chronicle begins in Hussite Prague when [[Alessandro di Ravello]] summons the coterie to investigate the destruction of [[Radomir Pavlovich]]. Radomir's haven points in several directions at once: revenant blood, missing ritual books, Tzimisce relics, Tremere correspondence, Hussite radicalism, and signs of spirit protections. The investigation pulls the coterie toward [[Istvan Vlaszy]], the Premysl revenants, and the old Tzimisce-Tremere war under the city.

## Sabina, Tremere, And The Tzimisce Shadow

The coterie reaches [[Sabina Premysl]], only for a gargoyle attack and supernatural abduction to throw the investigation open. Sabina's disappearance, the Tremere request for retroactive Embrace permission, and [[Dragan Premysl]]'s testimony reveal that the Tremere are buying Cainites and cleaning up problems in Prague. Beneath that political layer, [[Shagra's Bloodline]] and the Tzimisce revenant network suggest a deeper land-bound intelligence moving through flesh, blood, and old obligations.

## Fireflower And Revolt

Through [[Jakub of Thessaloniki (Zakariya ibn Masud al-Muntazir)]] and [[Graziano de Veronese]], the coterie learns of the [[The Fireflower|Fireflower]]: a revolutionary rite that can break elder blood bonds and create a new bond between comrades. The idea shifts the campaign from investigation to revolt. The coterie begins choosing sides, testing elder targets, and deciding what freedom might cost.

## Orlok And The Night Of Chaos

The coterie bargains with hidden Salubri and carries out the [[Orlok Assassination]] at the [[Three Keys Tavern]]. [[Adrastos von Orlok]] falls, and the city tips into the [[Night of Chaos]]. At the [[Fireflower Anarch Camp]], [[Istvan Vlaszy]] is ritually Embraced by Jakub, [[Father Zemislav]] leads the [[First Fireflower Valderie]], and the group becomes the seed of a true pack.

## The Northern Road

With Prague burning politically and spiritually, the pack weighs one last plan involving [[Sabina as Tremere Leverage]] and the [[Tremere Weapon Against Shagra]]. Instead, the road north opens through the [[Norse Setite Anteaters]], [[Greg the Tremere]], and the strange land-spirit traditions of [[Devara]] and the [[Lithuanian Hearth Pact]]. The coterie learns that Tzimisce dominion over land may be a corrupted version of an older relationship between vampires, spirits, and place.

## Stockholm

The epilogue carries the pack through Estonia, St. Petersburg, Helsinki, and finally [[Stockholm]]. They survive the [[Niktuku Attack in Estonia]], spread Sabbat rites among Brujah and Lasombra contacts, hear that the Anarchs have lost and the Camarilla has formed, and watch Istvan survive the Assamite curse as one of the [[Unconquered Banu Haqim]]. Chronicle 1 ends with the pack in Stockholm, older, stranger, and fully Sabbat.
