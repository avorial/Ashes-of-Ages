# Late Market

## Overview

A late market marked on the Prague map as one of the places still plausibly open at night. The coterie uses it as a meeting point after Game 1.

## Important events

- Contacts are consulted here in [[Game 1]].
- The coterie regroups here in [[Game 2]].
- Characters buy tools, hunt, and exchange information before following the Istvan lead.
