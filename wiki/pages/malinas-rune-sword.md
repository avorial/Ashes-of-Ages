# Malina's Rune Sword

## Overview

After [[Malina]] steals [[Istvan Vlaszy]]'s shoes, Istvan wakes to find his sword replaced by a near-identical rune-carved weapon.

## Details

- The runes are in the language of Malina's people.
- The sword tries to move on its own when partially drawn.
- It appears hostile to vampires and to the coterie.
- During the Orlok attack it slides toward Istvan's allies, apparently misunderstanding who holds or threatens whom.

- [[Malina]]
- [[Orlok Assassination]]
