# Setite Ship North

## Overview

The northern Setites have a ship crewed by those who must serve them. They offer it as passage north after the [[Night of Chaos]].

## Route

- Prague escape route
- Lithuania and Teutonic lands
- Later northern domains and Scandinavia

## Important Events

- The coterie chooses the ship instead of returning deeply into Prague.
- In [[Game 14]], the boat must be portaged after [[Greg the Tremere]] leaves with the horses.
