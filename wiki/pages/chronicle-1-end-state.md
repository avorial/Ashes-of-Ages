# Chronicle 1 End State

## Pack Status

- The coterie is now a Sabbat pack, not merely a Prague coterie.
- [[Father Zemislav]] is the pack priest.
- [[Istvan Vlaszy]] is the likely pack leader after becoming Banu Haqim and surviving the curse.
- [[Katherine Karálová]] and [[Theodosia Byzantios]] are discussed as possible haven-guardians or abbots.
- The pack's bond begins with the [[First Fireflower Valderie]] and matures over the time jump.

## Prague

- [[Adrastos von Orlok]] is destroyed.
- Multiple elders are dead or destabilized.
- [[Night of Chaos]] marks the collapse of the old equilibrium.
- The Anarch cause burns brightly, but later news says the Anarchs lose the wider war and the Camarilla forms.
- [[What Happened to Prague]] gathers the city-specific aftermath.

## Major Supernatural Truths Learned

- The Tremere have a [[Tremere Weapon Against Shagra]].
- [[Sabina Premysl]] is Embraced by Tremere off screen but may retain Tzimisce/revenant vulnerabilities.
- The Fireflower can break elder blood bonds and form a new pack bond.
- Land spirits are real and can be enslaved, married, bargained with, or corrupted.
- Tzimisce land-dominion may imitate older pacts like the [[Lithuanian Hearth Pact]].

## Ending Position

- The pack reaches [[Stockholm]] after roughly a century of epilogue movement.
- Chronicle 2 begins with the pack integrating itself into the Stockholm court.
