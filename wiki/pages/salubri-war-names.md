# Salubri War Names

## Overview

The hidden Salubri refuse to give names freely, explaining that their people hide names as sacred and strategic things during a "War of Names."

## Details

- The speaker's third eye glows during the meeting.
- The Salubri reject insulting or propagandized language used by Tremere.
- They agree to help against [[Adrastos von Orlok]] because he hunts and enslaves Salubri.
- They frame aid as requiring payment: life for life.
