# Lithuanian Hearth Pact

## Overview

The Lithuanian Hearth Pact is the working name for [[Devara]]'s coven and its relationship with local land-spirits.

## Beliefs And Practices

- The land owns itself.
- Devara and her coven marry or unite with the land rather than ruling it as prince or king.
- Their protections are mutual bonds with spirits, not domination.
- They consider Tzimisce treatment of land-spirits to be enslavement.
- They say every land has a spirit unless that spirit has been destroyed, which is rare.

## Locations

- A grove with a central tree and multiple hanged men.
- Nearby lands in Lithuania; Devara mentions other covens a day's journey east and four days northwest.
