# Graziano de Veronese

## Overview

Graziano de Veronese is a Lasombra revolutionary introduced by [[Jakub of Thessaloniki (Zakariya ibn Masud al-Muntazir)]]. He presents the [[Fireflower and Proto-Vauldere]] as a path away from elder tyranny and the Crown's curse.

## Appears In

- [[Game 5]]
- [[Game 6]]

## Notes

- He says seven Cainites brought down the Lasombra founder.
- He believes breaking blood bonds will collapse elder power structures from within.
- [[Dalibor]] pledges support during the conversation.
