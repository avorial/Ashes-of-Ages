# Jewish Quarter

## Overview

The western part of Old Town. The transcript notes that Prague's Jewish quarter differs from many others because the gates are not locked.

## Laws and danger

- Feeding on Jews risks the wrath of [[Prince Josef Zvi]].
- Jews seen outside the gates without appearing to leave the city can be arrested.
