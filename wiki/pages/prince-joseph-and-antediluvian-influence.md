# Prince Joseph and Antediluvian Influence

## Overview

Game 4 reframes Radomir's secret Tremere dealings as possibly serving one of [[Prince Josef Zvi]]'s larger fears: that antediluvian or ancient vampiric influence was pressing on Prague and its ruler.

## Confirmed Details

- Mira says Radomir sought esoteric secrets that could help the Prince resist or understand antediluvian influence.
- The Librarian knows of the pressure on Prince Josef and the history behind his paranoia.
- The Prince may have benefited from Radomir's work without knowing its full criminal cost.

## Historical Context From The Librarian

- Josef once created or contacted a protector for Prague.
- That protector killed many vampires and disrupted rival networks.
- The previous prince tried to have Josef killed by day.
- Josef prepared for the attack and overthrew the old prince.
