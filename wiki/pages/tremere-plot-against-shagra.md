# Tremere Plot Against Shagra

## Overview

Late in Chronicle 1, the Anarch leaders reveal that the Tremere have built a weapon to strike the ancient Tzimisce force associated with [[Shagra's Bloodline]]. The pack considers whether [[Sabina Premysl]] can be used as leverage to make the Tremere deploy it before they flee Prague.
