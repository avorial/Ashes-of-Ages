# Katherine's Hidden Trunk

## Overview

After the [[Orlok Assassination]], [[Katherine Karalova]] returns to [[The Librarian's Library]], silences the room, packs important books into a waterproof trunk, and throws it into the river.

## Contents

The transcript does not list the exact titles. The trunk is described as containing important books or knowledge Katherine does not want to leave openly in the library.

## Later Discussion

In [[Game 13]], Katherine considers returning to recover the trunk before fleeing Prague. The group decides the box is likely stable enough to leave for later, especially because Rudolph or the maker seems to have built it well.
