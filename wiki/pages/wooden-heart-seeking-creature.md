# Wooden Heart-Seeking Creature

## Overview

During the aftermath of the Chantry raid, [[Dragan Premysl]] describes a wooden monstrosity that tried to drive itself into his heart.

## Lore

[[Istvan Vlaszy]] recalls old Koldunic tales of graveyard-tree roots possessed by the dead and animated by blood magic to hunt warm beating hearts.

## Uncertain

- The creature that attacked Dragan is related to, but not identical with, the wooden implement found in Radomir's murder investigation.
