# Gehenna Tapestry

## Overview

The Cappadocian negotiator offers a tapestry that produces visions of Gehenna when blood is poured on it. It is presented as a valuable Noddist or apocalyptic artifact.

## Found By / Connected To

- Offered to the coterie in [[Game 4]] as payment for suppressing [[Radomir's Cainite Sales to the Tremere]].
