# Stake the Librarian Offer

## Overview

After [[Katherine Karalova]] hides her books, a stranger approaches and asks her to stake [[The Librarian]] instead of killing him.

## Details

- The request is framed as preserving knowledge for later.
- The stranger's identity and allegiance are not confirmed in the transcript.
- The offer matters because Katherine has just acted against her sire's library and may be outside his trust.
