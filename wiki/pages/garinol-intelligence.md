# Garinol Intelligence

## Overview

[[Katherine Karalova]] researches [[Garinol]] while the coterie considers elder targets. The information paints him as a corpse-focused Cainite with a heretical monastic past and a strict feeding limitation.

## Confirmed Details

- Garinol was Embraced among a monastic order later found heretical in Prague.
- The heresy taught that the spirits of the dead remain in their bodies through purgatory until their sins burn away.
- The former monastery was later converted into a rich man's house.
- Garinol has acquired or controls a corpse-holding, abattoir-like place.
- The Nosferatu have an arrangement not to enter that place.
- Garinol cannot feed on the young, apparently meaning those eighteen or younger.
- His herd is among the bedridden elderly, especially in homes where older family members are left alone while others attend church.
- He has a marked interest in victims appearing dead while still alive.
