# Castle Catacombs Audience

## Overview

After the coterie shares what they have learned, [[The Librarian]] says [[Prince Josef Zvi]] may listen if they can show that his agents are driving Prague toward war rather than protecting it.

## Conditions

- The coterie should bring evidence that agents or "dogs" of the Prince are escalating conflict.
- Crusader-linked forces outside Prague may be a useful lever.
- The meeting is to be arranged in the [[Castle Catacombs]] the following night.

## Payment

[[Katherine Karalova]] gives the Librarian the [[Book of Koldun Exorcery]] as payment for the boon or meeting.
