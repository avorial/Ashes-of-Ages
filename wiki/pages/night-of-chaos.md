# Night of Chaos

## Overview

The Night of Chaos is the period after the coterie's attacks and Anarch plots leave multiple elders dead and Prague's Kindred power structure unstable.

## Confirmed Effects

- [[Adrastos von Orlok]] is destroyed.
- Several key elders are reported dead.
- Lasombra leadership is in chaos.
- Cappadocians are reportedly attacked by their own childer.
- Toreador rivals maneuver for power after Orlok's fall.
- The Anarchs prepare the [[Gunpowder Diversion at Elder Elysium]].
