# Spirit Communion Ritual

## Confirmed facts

- [[Sabina Premysl]] says [[Radomir Pavlovich]] received a package containing arcane rituals and runes.
- The rituals excited Radomir and warmed his blood.
- Radomir used Sabina as a vessel, filling her with spirits so he could interrogate them.
- Sabina describes the spirits as older than Christianity.
- She believes the ritual occupied Radomir and left his defenses lowered.
- The ritual circle Sabina later draws resembles the markings found at Radomir's haven.
- The ritual is interrupted by a gargoyle attack before the spirit witness can be questioned.

## Risks

- Spirit activity is explicitly dangerous.
- Istvan says a spirit's behavior is shaped by observation and awareness; more eyes may help, but antagonizing spirits remains dangerous.

## Game 3 notes

- The recreated ritual at Sabina's cottage is interrupted by the gargoyle and then overtaken by darker forces.
- The resulting shadow creature answers questions about Radomir's killer, but in cryptic terms.
