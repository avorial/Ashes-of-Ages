# Game 2

## Synopsis

The second session opens with the coterie regrouping after the first night's investigation. Dalibor's retainer has bought [[Radomir Pavlovich's Haven]], beating [[Istvan Vlaszy]] at auction. The group meets at the [[Late Market]], handles feeding and curfew concerns, and decides to follow the revenant lead rather than immediately pursue the remote castle tied to Istvan's courier work.

At [[Istvan Vlaszy's House]], the coterie learns that Istvan had been ferrying packages for Radomir without knowing their contents. One courier route involved meeting a page outside Prague and riding to a castle four hours north. Istvan points the group toward [[Dragan Premysl]], Radomir's enforcer. At [[Dragan Premysl's House]], Dragan confirms his loyalty to the dead master and bargains with the investigators. He directs them toward [[Sabina Premysl]], whose spirit-sight and ritual role make her a key witness.

At [[Sabina Premysl's Cottage]], the Gauntlet is dangerously thin. Theodosia is overwhelmed by nature spirits, Eliska experiences sorrow through synesthetic impressions, and the house is revealed as a laboratory of awakened or spirit-bound objects. Sabina explains that Radomir received a mysterious package containing arcane rituals and runes; he used her as a vessel to commune with old spirits, leaving himself vulnerable. The group agrees to let Sabina attempt to call the spirit witness again, but as she draws the circle, a gargoyle crashes through the roof and breaks the ward.

## Major events

- The group meets at the [[Late Market]] after the first night of investigation.
- Dalibor confirms that his retainers purchased [[Radomir Pavlovich's Haven]], outbidding [[Istvan Vlaszy]].
- The coterie learns that a separate arson attempt against the haven occurred after they left; a suspect is held at [[Castle Jail]].
- The coterie visits [[Istvan Vlaszy's House]], a transport business with an eagle-claw knocker.
- Istvan reveals that Radomir's packages went through him, including a route to a castle four hours north of Prague.
- The group visits [[Dragan Premysl's House]], where Dragan bargains and reveals that [[Sabina Premysl]] may have answers.
- A watcher or attacker follows the group from the neonate Elysium area and fires a crossbow bolt; the bolt traces back to German lands tied to crusader forces.
- At [[Sabina Premysl's Cottage]], the coterie experiences a place where the spirit world is extremely close.
- Sabina explains the mysterious package and Radomir's spirit-communion research.
- Sabina begins recreating the ritual circle when a gargoyle breaks through the roof and interrupts the rite.

## Clues and discoveries

- Istvan carried Radomir's packages but was not permitted to know their contents.
- One package route involved a page outside the city and a castle four hours north.
- Sabina says a wounded man from the Hussite fighting claimed the mystery package came from a crusader horse.
- The ritual package contained arcane rituals and runes that excited Radomir and warmed his blood.
- Radomir used Sabina as a vessel to commune with spirits older than Christianity.
- Sabina believes the ritual was bait: it occupied Radomir and left his defenses lowered.
- The Tremere have a castle near the road, and Istvan was told to avoid a direction because of Tremere influence.
- The Gauntlet at Sabina's cottage is extremely thin, and many objects in the house are awakened or spirit-bound.
- A gargoyle attacks just as Sabina begins the ritual to contact the spirit witness.
