# Orlok Assassination

## Overview

In [[Game 10]], the coterie attacks [[Adrastos von Orlok]] at the [[Three Keys Tavern]] with hidden Salubri aid.

## Event Summary

- [[Dalibor]] enters the gambling room where Orlok is playing dominoes.
- Orlok uses Majesty and exposes [[Istvan Vlaszy]] as tied to Tzimisce interests.
- A Tzimisce defender or construct in the room becomes blood or a living table-like horror.
- The Salubri ally lights a quarterstaff and intentionally rides frenzy to resist supernatural control.
- The Salubri's opened third eye makes melee wounds aggravated.
- [[Father Zemislav]] uses shadow tentacles after darkness returns.
- Orlok is destroyed, but a psychic observer escapes.

## Consequences

- The attack helps trigger the [[Night of Chaos]].
- The coterie flees to the [[Fireflower Anarch Camp]].
