# Prince Josef's Arm Ritual

## Overview

In [[Game 5]], [[Prince Josef Zvi]] demands [[Istvan Vlaszy]]'s right arm. He removes it, speaks in Aramaic, and burns the arm into tiny ruby-like crystals.

## Observed Result

- The ritual appears to extract or crystallize elements of Istvan's revenant blood.
- The coterie concludes that Josef has confirmed Istvan's true domitor or ancient bloodline connection.
- Istvan fears this exposes him to [[Shagra]] and may endanger his family.

## Uncertain

- The exact ritual tradition is not identified.
- Whether Josef learned something new or confirmed a suspicion is unclear.
