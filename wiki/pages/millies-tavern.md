# Millie's Tavern

## Overview

A three-story tavern near the bridge. In Game 1 it is unusually quiet for early night and has two soldiers outside who do not stop the coterie.

## Important events

- [[Alessandro di Ravello]] summons the coterie here.
- The investigation into [[Radomir Pavlovich]]'s death begins here.
