# Castle Jail

## Overview

A jail in the castle area. In Game 1/Game 2, the coterie learns that a suspect in the arson attempt against [[Radomir Pavlovich's Haven]] was captured and taken here.

## Open questions

- Whether the suspect actually started the fire is uncertain.
- The fire occurred after the coterie left the haven and is separate from Father Zemislav's statue incident.
