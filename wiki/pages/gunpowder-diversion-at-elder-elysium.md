# Gunpowder Diversion at Elder Elysium

## Overview

The Anarchs plan to ignite or explode stores near the elders' private meeting place to draw reinforcements, kill additional elders if possible, and cover any move against Shagra's flesh room.

## Details

- The target is described as an elder meeting hall near the castle, called the Castle of Sand in [[Game 13]].
- The explosion is expected to turn the place into glass or otherwise devastate it with fire.
- In [[Game 13]], a distant explosion confirms that the plan has begun while the coterie is at the Anarch camp.
