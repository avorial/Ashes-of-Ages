# Red Calf

## Overview

The Red Calf is an active tavern with roughly twenty people present during Game 3. The crossbow trail from Elysium leads the coterie here.

## Important Events

- The coterie finds Jacob sitting with an unusual metallic christening-like goblet.
- Jacob says he attempted to liberate [[Istvan Vlaszy]] rather than murder him.
- The conversation introduces the [[Blood Bond and the Crown]] and [[Fireflower and Proto-Vauldere]].
