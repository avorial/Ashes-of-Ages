# Boat That Sailed on Land

## Overview

In [[Game 13]], [[Greg the Tremere]] rips out a dying crusader's heart, lets it turn to dust over the boat, and causes the vessel to float over the ground as if the earth were water.

## Significance

- The working demonstrates Greg's extreme Tremere power.
- It allows the carriage and boat to continue travel after the ambush.
- It foreshadows the stranger forms of land magic encountered in [[Game 14]].
