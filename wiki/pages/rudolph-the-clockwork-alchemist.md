# Rudolph the Clockwork Alchemist

## Overview

Rudolph is a wanted alchemist and engineering-minded contact of [[Katherine Karalova]]. Guards at the [[Night Market]] seek a young alchemist they consider a heretic to God's order. Katherine later finds Rudolph wounded in a Nosferatu oubliette.

## Appears In

- [[Game 9]]
- [[Game 10]]

## Details

- He survives in darkness by drawing engineering layouts and planning escape.
- He has clockwork engineering visible under or within his ribcage.
- Katherine ghouls him and he understands that she is undead.
- He asks for help reaching a safe place against his opposition.

## Relationships

- [[Katherine Karalova]] - rescuer, patron, and domitor after she ghouls him.
