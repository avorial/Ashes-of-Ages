# Dragan's Szlachta Work

## Overview

In [[Game 9]], [[Istvan Vlaszy]] finds [[Dragan Premysl]] in the basement of [[Dragan Premysl's House]] reshaping six tied prisoners into szlachta-like servants.

## Details

- The prisoners are alive and restrained.
- Dragan alters bones and hands into blades.
- Dragan is calmer than before but says he wakes with mania to perform tasks that do not feel wholly his own.
- He believes his will and form are being influenced by spirits or "true masters."

## Open Questions

- Whether Dragan is acting under Shagra's pressure, wider Tzimisce compulsion, or another spirit influence is not fully resolved in the transcript.
