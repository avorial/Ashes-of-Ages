# Tremere-Tzimisce War

## Confirmed facts

- Radomir's correspondence contains repeated aggression toward Clan Tremere and arguments that they should be purged.
- Radomir pressed [[Prince Josef Zvi]] to expel the Tremere from Prague.
- The prince refused to facilitate such violence and stated that he had given the Tremere domain.
- Radomir was searching for the Tremere's primary chantry.
- In Game 2, Istvan recognizes that a route connected to Radomir's packages passes near a Tremere-influenced castle area.

## Unconfirmed

- The transcripts make Tremere involvement a strong suspicion, but do not prove that Tremere killed Radomir.

## Game 3-4 notes

- Dragan and Mira reveal that Radomir secretly traded Cainites or bodies to the Tremere for war magic and esoteric knowledge.
- Sabina's suspected Tremere abduction and the red-capped war wagons attacking a Tzimisce castle both escalate the conflict.
- Game 4 research identifies the red caps as Tremere-linked servants or messengers.
