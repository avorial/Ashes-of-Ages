# Blood Bond and the Crown

## Overview

At the [[Red Calf]], Jakub describes the blood bond as a chain associated with the Crown or Crone from Noddist myth. He says Cain broke the bond after a year and a day, and that younger vampires remain trapped in versions of those chains.

## Confirmed Table Lore

- Jakub presents the blood bond as a tool of vampiric hierarchy and enslavement.
- He connects liberation from the bond to recent upheavals among Lasombra, Tzimisce, and Assamite/Banu Haqim actors.
- This is delivered as Jakub's doctrine and should be treated as his framing, not neutral cosmic fact.
