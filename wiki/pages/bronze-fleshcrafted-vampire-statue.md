# Bronze Fleshcrafted Vampire Statue

## Overview

A bronze-like statue shaped from a living vampire, found in a trunk or room at [[Radomir Pavlovich's Haven]]. The figure's arms and legs curve into tentacle-like forms, and its head is flattened into a platform. The transcript states it is still alive or in torpor-like condition, with micro-movements detectable.

## Important events

- Found in [[Game 1]].
- Father Zemislav covers it with a sheet and removes it from the haven.
- A demonic or abyssal manifestation confronts him while he is moving it.

## Open questions

- Who was this vampire before fleshcrafting?
- Why was it kept at Radomir's haven?
- What happens if it is restored or awakened?
