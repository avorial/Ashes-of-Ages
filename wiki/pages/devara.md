# Devara

## Overview

Devara is a vampire and land-wife encountered in [[Game 14]] after the coterie crosses into Lithuanian lands.

## Appears In

- [[Game 14]]

## Details

- She wears a Slavic wedding gown.
- Her spirit-protection markings resemble Tzimisce runes but use a different alphabet and a different relationship to spirits.
- Her shoes remain clean regardless of mud.
- She describes the land as its own husband and master rather than a thing to be ruled.
- Her coven offers hospitality through the [[Hanged Men Blood Hospitality]].

## Relationships

- [[Lithuanian Hearth Pact]] - Devara's coven and magical tradition.
- [[Greg the Tremere]] - Greg previously mentioned hunting heretical witches in Lithuania, likely connected to this kind of group.

## Uncertain

- Clan or bloodline is not explicitly confirmed. The transcript suggests a Tremere-adjacent or heretical Tremere connection, but does not settle it.
