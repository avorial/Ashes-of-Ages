# Shadow Creature at Sabina's Cottage

## Overview

After [[Father Zemislav]]'s abyssal power and [[Sabina Premysl]]'s ritual interact, a shadow-like creature forms in the cottage. It answers several questions before the scene ends.

## Answers Given

- Radomir was killed by "darkness."
- The killer is not in the room.
- The killer is connected to "the ones who kill."
- The speaker frames the connection as analogous to enslavement: as the coterie is bound to those seeking the killer, the killer is connected to those who kill.

## Interpretations

- [[Istvan Vlaszy]] takes the answer as pointing toward crusaders or crusader-linked agents.
- Later events at the [[Red Calf]] connect the crossbow shooter to liberation rhetoric rather than a straightforward crusader command structure.
