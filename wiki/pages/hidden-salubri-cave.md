# Hidden Salubri Cave

## Overview

[[Dragan Premysl]] gives [[Istvan Vlaszy]] directions to a cave system near the river, roughly eight hundred yards from Prague's walls, that leads toward old mines and the Salubri refuge.

## Important Events

- The coterie follows coded left-right directions into the cave.
- Three Salubri warriors confront them.
- The caves appear to blunt the Tremere elder's remote flesh-control influence.
- The deeper route reaches the [[Labyrinth Chasm]].
- After leaving, the coterie discovers [[Carrion Footprints at the River]].
