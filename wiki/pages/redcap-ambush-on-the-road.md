# Redcap Ambush on the Road

## Overview

While traveling north in [[Game 13]], the coterie and Setites encounter a road ambush involving crusader attackers and Tremere redcaps around a carriage.

## Details

- Redcaps try to destroy or harry the carriage.
- The attackers are routed or killed during the encounter.
- [[Greg the Tremere]] emerges from the carriage and reveals far more power than his casual name suggests.
