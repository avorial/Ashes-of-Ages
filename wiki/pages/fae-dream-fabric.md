# Fae Dream Fabric

## Overview

[[Eliska von Vlasak]] purchases expensive fabric that causes a physical reaction when she touches it, making her hair stand up in a way she has not felt since her Embrace.

## Library Findings

- The fabric was made by fae or faith folk.
- It is solid in both dream and physical reality.
- It resists magical alteration, described in the transcript as resistance to being changed by "symmetry" or a similar term.
- The persons associated with it include a noble of their people and a servant, but the transcript names are uncertain.
