# Labyrinth Chasm

## Overview

In the deep cave near the hidden Salubri, [[Theodosia Byzantios]] and [[Father Zemislav]] investigate a chasm with no meaningful shroud between the living world and the Labyrinth.

## Confirmed Effects

- Theodosia glimpses something like the upper crust of Hell or Oblivion.
- The experience damages her Path and leaves her eyes black-on-black.
- Zemislav serves as a dark guide through the experience.

## Interpretation

The transcript treats the chasm as genuinely supernatural and dangerous. It does not fully explain why it is there or whether it is tied to Salubri defenses, Tzimisce land-magic, or older underworld wounds.
