# Gregory's Raid Warning

## Overview

At the [[Three Keys Tavern]], [[Gregory]] tells the coterie that a raid is planned in three days against forces outside Prague. He believes the elders fear the groups beyond the walls.

## Use

This warning becomes a possible timing point for the coterie's own revolutionary action.
