# Tremere Origin Confession

## Overview

[[Greg the Tremere]] gives [[Katherine Karalova]] and the group a frank explanation of Tremere origins and hubris.

## Claims

- The Tremere compelled Tzimisce vitae into their own Embrace.
- They altered Tzimisce vitae to be independent of the spiritual Vicissitude taint.
- They then took power connected to the Salubri and inherited or inflicted part of that curse.
- Greg frames Tremere arrogance as real and dangerous, not merely a political accusation.

## Caution

These are Greg's claims. The transcript treats him as knowledgeable, but this page should not assume he gives a complete or unbiased history.
