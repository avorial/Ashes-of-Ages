# Game 6

## Synopsis

Game 6 continues outside the walls with [[Graziano de Veronese]]. He explains his vision of freedom from the blood bond, using the destruction of the Lasombra founder as proof that even the oldest tyrannies can fall. [[Dalibor]] pledges support, while others remain wary of replacing one binding with another.

The group presses Graziano on what the Fireflower actually does. He says the Crown's bond is so loud that the victim cannot hear themselves think, while the Fireflower creates many voices so one's own will can be heard among them. He claims masters do not automatically know when the bond is broken.

After returning to Prague, [[Theodosia Byzantios]] prays over stored blood from Radomir's haven and is overwhelmed by the presence of a Tzimisce Methuselah. She claws at herself trying to remove the influence and later reaches the closed [[Night Market]] in a near-hysterical state, insisting that the ancient one's seeds are in the city, the people, the blood, and the skin.

The coterie takes her into a nearby church, where they meet [[Ecaterina the Wise]]. Ecaterina calms Theodosia with her blood and reveals a Promethean vision of vampires and humans living in mutual stewardship rather than predation. Her childe [[Cosimus]] recognizes [[Jardani Jovonovich]] as connected to the Prometheans.
