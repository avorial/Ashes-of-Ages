# Prince Josef's Abomination Commission

## Overview

During the private audience in [[Game 5]], [[Prince Josef Zvi]] accepts that [[Jakub of Thessaloniki (Zakariya ibn Masud al-Muntazir)]] killed [[Radomir Pavlovich]], then redirects the coterie toward the Tremere.

## Charge

- Determine whether Tremere abominations are mere trinkets, entertainments, experiments, or a threat to Prague.
- Keep the information contained enough to preserve court stability.
- Expect other factions to be pursuing the same information.

## Reward

The Prince implies renown or prestation may follow if the coterie brings useful proof.
