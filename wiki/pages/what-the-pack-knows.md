# What The Pack Knows

## About Prague

- The city's elder order was fragile before the coterie struck.
- [[Radomir Pavlovich]]'s death was not an isolated murder.
- The Tremere were buying, transforming, and weaponizing Cainites.
- The Tzimisce revenant network is tied to older land and blood influences.

## About The Fireflower

- The Fireflower can break elder blood bonds.
- It can create a new bond between participants.
- The rite is not merely political; it produces visions and spiritual consequences.
- [[First Fireflower Valderie]] is the pack's founding rite.

## About Shagra And Land

- Shagra's influence works through flesh, blood, revenants, and possibly the land itself.
- The Tremere have a weapon designed to strike Shagra's psyche.
- [[Devara]]'s coven shows that vampires can relate to land-spirits through mutual pacts instead of domination.

## About The North

- The [[Norse Setite Anteaters]] carry Fireflower north.
- [[Cain's Northern Tomb]] may exist, but its truth is unconfirmed.
- [[Stockholm]] is the next meaningful stage of the pack's story.
