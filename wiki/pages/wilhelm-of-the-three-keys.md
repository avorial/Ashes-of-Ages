# Wilhelm of the Three Keys

## Overview

Wilhelm is the innkeeper of the [[Three Keys Tavern]]. The mortal sheriff tells [[Dalibor]] that Wilhelm imprisoned and assaulted a woman, then forced her father to permit a marriage.

## Notes

- Dalibor investigates Wilhelm as part of his arrangement with the mortal sheriff.
- The coterie later works to make Wilhelm lose control of the tavern through a rigged chance or gambling scheme.
