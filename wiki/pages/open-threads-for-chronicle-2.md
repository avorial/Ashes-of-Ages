# Open Threads for Chronicle 2

## Personal Threads

- [[Theodosia Byzantios]] still carries the consequences of [[Theodosia's Tzimisce Infection]] and the [[Labyrinth Chasm]].
- [[Katherine Karálová]] left [[Katherine's Hidden Trunk]] in the river near Prague.
- [[Istvan Vlaszy]] has Banu Haqim guidance, the legacy of revenant servitude, and the status of [[Unconquered Banu Haqim]].
- The pack must settle final roles after the time jump. See [[Pack Roles After Prague]].

## Political Threads

- The Camarilla exists by the time the pack reaches the north.
- The Sabbat identity is now explicit for the pack.
- [[Stockholm]] is the next court to infiltrate or reshape.
- The [[Norse Setite Anteaters]] and other northern converts may become allies, rivals, or converts.

## Mythic Threads

- [[Cain's Northern Tomb]] remains unresolved.
- [[Land Spirits and Tzimisce Dominion]] becomes a major frame for future conflicts.
- [[Tremere Origin Confession]] and [[Greg the Tremere]] leave open what the Tremere antitribu future may mean.
