# Ari

## Overview

Ari is a mortal servant of [[Prince Josef Zvi]]. He escorts the coterie to the Prince's private audience in [[Game 5]].

## Notes

- He is recovering from a leg injury caused by a crowd leaving the mock burial.
- His aura appears unusually clear and crisp.
- The coterie suspects some kind of possession or supernatural alteration.
- He uses a hand-wave compulsion to pass the Jewish Quarter gate.
