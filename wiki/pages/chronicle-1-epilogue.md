# Chronicle 1 Epilogue

## Overview

The end of [[Game 14]] compresses roughly a century after the flight from Prague.

## Route

- Prague falls into the [[Night of Chaos]].
- The pack travels north with the [[Norse Setite Anteaters]].
- They meet [[Devara]] and the [[Lithuanian Hearth Pact]].
- They spend years in northern regions and survive the [[Niktuku Attack in Estonia]].
- In [[St. Petersburg Sabbat Work]], they convert Brujah and manipulate mortal nobility.
- In [[Helsinki and the Camarilla News]], they learn the Anarchs lost and the Camarilla formed.
- At sea, [[Istvan Vlaszy]] survives the Assamite curse and becomes one of the [[Unconquered Banu Haqim]].
- The pack lands in [[Stockholm]], the starting point for Chronicle 2.

## Character State

- The coterie is now unambiguously Sabbat.
- [[Father Zemislav]] is the pack priest.
- [[Istvan Vlaszy]] is discussed as a likely pack leader.
- [[Katherine Karálová]] or [[Theodosia Byzantios]] are discussed as possible abbots or haven-guardians.
