# First Fireflower Valderie

## Overview

[[Father Zemislav]] leads the coterie and Anarch allies through the first Fireflower Valderie using the [[Silver Fireflower Chalice]].

## Details

- Blood placed in the chalice produces a true flower of flame.
- [[Theodosia Byzantios]]'s tainted blood recoils or crawls back rather than joining cleanly.
- Participants experience visions of blood, flame, Cain, Lilith, the Crone, and freedom from elder chains.
- The rite forms Viniculum bonds that become the foundation for the pack.
