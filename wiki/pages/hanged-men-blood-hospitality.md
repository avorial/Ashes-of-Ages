# Hanged Men Blood Hospitality

## Overview

At [[Devara]]'s grove, hanged men are used to provide blood and magic-infused hospitality to the visiting vampires.

## Details

- Four hanged men hang from a central tree in different states of decay.
- The bodies are people who died for particular crimes.
- Their deaths allow magic to funnel through their bodies.
- Devara gathers blood from one hanged man's foot into a cup.
- The blood tastes cold and gritty, like unwashed vegetable soup, and gives substantial nourishment.
