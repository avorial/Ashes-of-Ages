# Radomir's Missing Ritual Book

## Overview

A missing book from [[Radomir Pavlovich's Haven]]. The investigators find a space behind a bookcase or cabinet where a book had been chained or locked, but the book itself is gone.

## Later connection

In [[Game 2]], [[Sabina Premysl]] says Radomir received a book/package containing rituals and runes that he used for spirit communion. This is likely connected to the missing book, but the transcript does not prove whether it is the same object.
