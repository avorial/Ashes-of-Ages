# Carrion Footprints at the River

## Overview

After leaving the [[Hidden Salubri Cave]], the coterie finds tracks near the riverbank.

## Clue Details

- The tracks appear to belong to an adult male.
- The scent is carrion-like or corpse-like.
- The figure seems to have come from the water, followed or watched the group, and not gone as deep into the caves.
- The coterie suspects [[Garinol]], but the transcript does not confirm the identity.
