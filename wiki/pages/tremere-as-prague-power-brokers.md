# Tremere as Prague Power Brokers

## Overview

After the Prince's audience in [[Game 5]], [[Adrastos von Orlok]] tells the coterie that the Tremere are the court's major power brokers. No one would follow a Tremere prince, but whoever the Tremere support can force others to bow.

## Implications

- Tremere support has benefited other factions in different eras.
- Their war chest and preparation against the Tzimisce makes them difficult to oppose directly.
- Their role helps explain why the court tolerates behavior that younger Cainites find monstrous.
