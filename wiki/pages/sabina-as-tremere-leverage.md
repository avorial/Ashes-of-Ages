# Sabina as Tremere Leverage

## Overview

The group reasons that [[Sabina Premysl]], Embraced by the Tremere off screen, may be able to convince the Tremere to use the [[Tremere Weapon Against Shagra]].

## Logic

- Sabina is Tremere now, but her old revenant body and Tzimisce-crafted nature may still transmit Shagra's control.
- The Tremere may flee Prague unless convinced Shagra can track them anywhere.
- Sabina could frame weapon use as necessary survival rather than a favor to the Anarchs.

## Uncertain

- The transcript does not show this plan being carried out.
