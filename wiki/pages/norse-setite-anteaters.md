# Norse Setite Anteaters

## Overview

The Norse Setite Anteaters are a northern heretical Setite band encountered in the [[Fireflower Anarch Camp]]. Their ancestors fled Egypt and Europe into lands cold enough that no snakes lived there.

## Details

- Their name is given as "the anteaters."
- They are Setites, but their magic is intertwined with northern spirits, sleeping dragons, and cold lands.
- [[Svigar Derkinson]] says they were sent south by an enslaving leader to trade northern artifacts.
- After receiving the Fireflower, they intend to return north and overthrow their own masters.
- They offer the coterie passage on the [[Setite Ship North]].
