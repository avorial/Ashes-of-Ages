# Graziano's Revolution

## Overview

[[Graziano de Veronese]] meets the coterie outside Prague after [[Jakub of Thessaloniki (Zakariya ibn Masud al-Muntazir)]] brings them to him. He says he helped destroy the Lasombra founder and now seeks to spread freedom from the Crown's curse.

## Philosophy

- Elder rule depends on blood bonds, fear, and habit.
- Breaking enough bonds will make dependent power structures collapse.
- Freedom means choosing service and association rather than being compelled by false love.
- He does not claim the future will be free of all hierarchy, only that the uniquely vampiric whip of the blood bond can be broken.
