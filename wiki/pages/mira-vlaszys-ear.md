# Mira Vlaszy's Ear

## Overview

The Cappadocian negotiator produces an ear he says belongs to [[Mira Vlaszy]] in order to threaten [[Istvan Vlaszy]]. Mira is later found alive with both ears.

## Explanation

Mira says the ear was handed over earlier as part of [[Radomir Pavlovich]]'s Tremere arrangements, likely to pass blood wards or prove access.
