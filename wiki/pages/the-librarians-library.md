# The Librarian's Library

## Overview

The Librarian's Library is a public-facing research space where the coterie studies dangerous or obscure topics.

## Important Events

- [[Eliska von Vlasak]] researches the [[Fae Dream Fabric]].
- [[Father Zemislav]] researches earlier prince-related nightmares and antediluvian influence.
- [[Istvan Vlaszy]] researches [[Necromancer Countermeasures]].
- [[The Librarian]] explains [[Prince Joseph and Antediluvian Influence]] and arranges the [[Castle Catacombs Audience]].
