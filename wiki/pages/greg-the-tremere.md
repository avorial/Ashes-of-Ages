# Greg the Tremere

## Overview

Greg is a powerful Tremere encountered on the road north in [[Game 13]]. He gives only the casual name "Greg" and claims to come from Baku or a distant inland sea region.

## Appears In

- [[Game 13]]
- [[Game 14]]

## Confirmed Details

- He carries a walking stick with Hermetic runes, bones, and fetishes.
- His aura is dark and occultly burdened.
- He claims never to have been in Prague.
- He is being attacked or manipulated by other Tremere interests.
- He demonstrates elemental mastery by making a boat travel over land after turning a dying crusader's heart to dust.
- He speaks about hunting heretical witches in Lithuania.

## Uncertain

- Out-of-character table talk suggests a famous Tremere identity, but the in-character transcript only confirms "Greg."
